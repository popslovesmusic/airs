### What you do

# Path 3 — **Field Dynamics Exploration (SATP)**

### What you do

* Explore different initial conditions:

  * random noise
  * gradients
  * multiple perturbations
* Run medium-length evolutions (1k–10k steps)
* Observe:

  * damping vs amplification
  * pattern formation
  * dimensional differences

### Benefits

* Builds intuition about SATP behavior
* May reveal emergent structures
* Prepares SATP for later physical interpretation
* Still safely within numerical validation

### Costs

* Data-heavy
* Can produce ambiguous patterns
* Risk of “simulation wandering” without hypotheses

### Best if

You want **emergence and dynamics**, not semantics.

# Path 5 — **Theoretical Formalization (Write, Don’t Code)**

### What you do

### Costs

* Requires mental discipline instead of experimentation

### Best if

You want to **lock meaning** before adding motion.
